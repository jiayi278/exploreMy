Overview Festival in Malaysia

The festivals in Malaysia reflect the country's rich cultural diversity and heritage, showcasing a vibrant tapestry of traditions, customs, and celebrations. Malaysia is home to multiple ethnic groups, including Malay, Chinese, Indian, and indigenous communities, each contributing unique festivals and cultural practices to the country's festive calendar.
The coexistence of these different ethnicities has created a rich tapestry of cultural celebrations, with each group celebrating their respective religious and cultural festivals. These festivals serve as important markers in the social and religious calendars of the communities, providing occasions for family gatherings, religious observance, and community bonding.
